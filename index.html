About us

We are the gurudwara family

Welcome to Gurudwara, a house of worship open to all seeking spiritual growth and peace. Our Gurdwara serves the community by providing a welcoming space for prayer, meditation, and learning about Sikhism. We hold daily langar, a free communal meal, where everyone can gather and share a simple meal together. We believe in seva, selfless service, and encourage all visitors to participate in acts of kindness within the Gurdwara or their own communities. If you're new to Sikhism, feel free to ask questions! We're here to share the teachings of Guru Nanak Dev Ji and promote understanding and equality.
